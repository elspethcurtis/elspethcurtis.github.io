Welcome to my site! My name is Elspeth Curtis, I am based out of Louisville, KY and for the past year have been learning about web development.

As an Operations Coordinator, I had the opportunity to help build a Squarespace website. This whet my appetite and led me to Code Louisville at Bellarmine University.
